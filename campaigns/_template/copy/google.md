# Google ad copy

Replace `<PFX>` with the campaign's uppercase prefix (e.g. `LS` for leave-stainless, `FVS` for fern-vs-speakeasy). See [campaigns/README.md → Variant IDs](../../README.md#variant-ids).

Google Ads character limits:
- RSA headline: 30 max (provide 5-15)
- RSA description: 90 max (provide up to 4)
- Display short headline: 30 max
- Display long headline: 90 max
- Display description: 90 max
- Sitelink text: 25 max
- Sitelink description: 35 max each, two lines
